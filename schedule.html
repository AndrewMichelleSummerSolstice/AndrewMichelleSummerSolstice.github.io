Wedding Day Schedule

Everything you need to know for our Summer Solstice celebration

  1. 🌿

    Doors open · 6:00 PM

    Guests Arrive & Be Seated

    Doors open at 6:00 PM. Please find your seat using the seating chart before the ceremony begins. Ushers will be available to help.

  2. 💍

    Ceremony · 6:00 PM

    Wedding Ceremony

    Andrew and Michelle exchange their vows as the Summer Solstice sun begins to glow.

    The ceremony is expected to last approximately 30 minutes.

  3. 🥂

    Cocktail Hour · 6:30 – 7:30 PM

    Cocktail Hour

    Celebrate with cocktails, passed appetizers, and good company while we capture our first moments as husband and wife. Explore, mingle, and enjoy!

  4. 🎊

    Reception · 7:30 PM

    Reception Begins

    Guests are invited into the reception hall. Enjoy dinner, toasts from our closest family and friends, and dancing all night long.

    First dances · Dinner service · Toasts · Cake cutting · Open dancing

  5. 🌙

    Last Dance · 11:15 PM

    Last Dance

    Join us on the dance floor for our final song of the night.

  6. ✨

    Send-Off · 11:30 PM

    Grand Send-Off

    Help us close out the night with a memorable send-off as the evening comes to a close. Safe travels home — we love you all!
