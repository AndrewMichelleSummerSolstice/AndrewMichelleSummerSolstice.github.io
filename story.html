Prelude

The Perm Saga

It started, as many great love stories do, with a haircut. In December 2023, Andrew convinced his brother Daniel — a medical student with untapped style potential — to get a perm. The pitch was straightforward: a fresh look would win over the girl he’d been hoping to impress.

The plan worked brilliantly. Daniel’s new curls were an immediate hit, so much so that his classmates were overheard saying, “He has better hair than me.” The Perm Saga had officially become legend.

Grateful for the unsolicited — but undeniably effective — styling advice, Daniel decided to return the favor. He introduced Andrew to Michelle, and the rest is the story you’re reading right now.

"I tried dating apps, meeting people, even speed dating — but the moment I met Michelle, I knew she was the one, even if she lived on the other side of the country."

— Andrew

Chapter One

How We Met

On New Year’s Day 2024, Andrew and Michelle crossed paths for the first time in a group setting — and it wouldn’t be the last. Over the following week, they kept finding themselves at the same board game nights, where Michelle and her friends made sure Andrew was well acquainted with the feeling of losing.

Between game nights, the two explored a little further afield — wandering through the American Dream Mall, checking out the MrBeast Burger, and finishing the day over boba. By the end of it all, Andrew had extended an open invitation: Michelle and her friends were always welcome to visit him in Seattle.

Chapter Two

Becoming Official

A few months later, Michelle made her way to Seattle for Sakura Con — an anime convention that would turn out to be far more than just a weekend trip. She arrived a couple of days early, and for those first few days it was just the two of them.

Andrew, naturally, came prepared. He deployed the full Seattle welcome: free sodas courtesy of Microsoft, a carefully curated tour of the city’s best food spots, and enough charm to make a convincing case for the Pacific Northwest. By the time Sakura Con officially kicked off, something between them had quietly shifted.

They made it official. And one month later, Michelle packed up and moved to Seattle — fully, completely all in.
